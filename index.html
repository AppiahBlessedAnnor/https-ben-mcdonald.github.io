Project Idea

Overview

The project is an app I like to call "Hive Mind". The idea is to have an app that can automate all other apps on your phone or computer. I am aware that Apple has created a similar application before, but only to a certain extent. What I would like to design with this app is something that can follow and execute exact commands that have been preset by you, for a time that you have designated it to launch, without you being present. In addition to this key feature of automation, I plan to design the app to learn the user's habits. I call this feature "advanced learning". More on that later, though.

Motivation

The idea for this app comes from my unfortunate tendency to forget to do something five minutes after thinking to my self, "I need to remember to do that". A lot of the time I need to set reminders for myself which can be annoying when you constantly have to do it. I know I'm not the only person who has this problem. People forget to do simple things every day. Whether it's forgetting to send an important text or E-mail because they're busy, or they forget to sign into their work app because they're running late. It happens all the time. Hive Mind is an app that will solve this problem with just a few taps.

Description

Hive Mind will be able to control all your other apps without you having to be there. The premis is to have Hive Mind simulate your choices by pre-recording your selections. This is the simplest method of automation. All you have to do is click on the Hive Mind app. Hive Mind will open up a replica of your home screen or desktop. Then you simply click on the application you want to automate and show what you want Hive Mind to do by clicking through the process as if you were doing the command (Don't worry, this is just a simulation of your desktop so the action won't actually work, feel free to click away). This is so Hive Mind can store your choices and replicate them later. When your done, click the finish button and set the time you would like the automation to be activated.

Next is a handy little feature I like to call "Learner". This feature learns and remembers reoccuring automations such as setting alarms, signing in and out of work, and sending reoccuring E-mails. "Learner" notices when you select the same automation three times and asks if you would like to make this a reoccuring automation. Learner can also be activated and set from the settings panel so you dont have to wait until you've activated the same automation three times.

The final feature is called "Higher Learning". This feature can be taught to read text from calender apps and set automations automatically. For example, if your work app posts next week's shifts Higher learning will react to the time and date of each shift and set the automation for sign in and sign out, as well as set alarms to wake you up based on your preferences. Higher leaarning will be an update for later as there is a lot that has to go into it. I want to make sure this part of the program works as advertised before releasing it. There will of course be online suport to help with any issues as well as an FAQ to help with any issues offline. On top of that there will be a setup wizard to help users get started and take them on a tour of the features. In time I will implement new ideas to improve Hive Mind, though I feel as though the three main features will be a good starting point. Hive Mind is a simple automation app for a simpler life.
